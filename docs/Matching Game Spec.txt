Matching Game

Two players take turns revealing cards. 
There are 2 sets of ten cards.
To reveal cards, players click on the buttons that represent cards.
When clicked on, the button should reveal a card.
A Player picks 2 cards on his/her turn.
If no match is found, it's the next players turn, and the cards go face down again.
If a match is found, that players score goes up 1, the player plays another turn, and the cards should stay revealed.
Game goes until all matches are found.
At the beginning and end of the game, clicks should not do anything.

Throughout the game, there should be messages based on when matches are found, who wins, etc.
Before the game starts, it should say click start to play.
